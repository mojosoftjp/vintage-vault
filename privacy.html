Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

VINTAGE VAULT respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your data stays on your device.

Overview

VINTAGE VAULT ("the App") is a personal catalog application for managing your collection of vintage guitars, amplifiers, and pedals. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. The App does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts
  • Monitor your usage
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Require account creation or login

Data Storage

All data you enter into the App—including photos, gear information, purchase history, and notes—is stored locally on your device only. This data is:

  • Never uploaded to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never accessible to us or anyone else
  • Completely under your control

Photo Access

The App requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to select images for your gear collection. We:

  • Only access photos you explicitly select
  • Store selected photos locally within the App
  • Never upload photos to any server
  • Never share photos with any third party

Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads may use device identifiers to serve relevant content. You can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings. We do not have access to any data collected by the advertising network.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases to remove advertisements. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual items within the App
  • Uninstalling the App (this will remove all data)

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is designed for general audiences and does not contain any content inappropriate for children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please contact us at:

support@example.com

Or visit our Support page for more information.

Your collection is yours alone. We believe your data belongs to you, and we've designed VINTAGE VAULT with that principle at its core.
