Frequently Asked Questions

How many photos can I add per item?

You can add up to 10 photos for each gear item. Photos can be added when creating a new item or when editing an existing one.

Is my data backed up?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, your data will be deleted. We recommend keeping your own photo backups. Cloud sync is planned for a future update.

Can I export my collection?

Export functionality is coming in a future update. This will include options to export as PDF, CSV, or image gallery.

How do I remove ads?

You can remove all advertisements by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option in Settings. This is a one-time purchase that unlocks the ad-free experience permanently.

What's the random splash feature?

When enabled in Settings, the app will randomly select one of your gear photos to display on the launch screen each time you open the app. It's a fun way to rediscover your collection!

Can I use this for other gear like keyboards or microphones?

Currently, the app supports guitars, amps, and pedals. More categories are planned for future updates based on user feedback.

Is there an iPad version?

The app works on iPad, but is currently optimized for iPhone. A dedicated iPad layout with multi-column support is planned for a future update.

My app is crashing. What should I do?

Please try the following: 1) Make sure you have the latest version of the app from the App Store. 2) Restart your device. 3) If the issue persists, please contact us with details about when the crash occurs.
